Description

Our website will display employment data for certain college majors when the user selects a category of major from drop-down list. This will allow users to find out what majors they would want to take and what are the chances of getting a job by taking that major.

motivating questions

  • Which major in this section will offer the most full-time positions?
  • Which major in this category has the highest unemployment rate
  • What majors could I take if I am intrested (insert category)?

Desirable features, with time estimates

When a specific category is requested, display:

  • a table of data on all majors pertainting to that category in the order of median salary Time: 1 hour

  • The Average unemployment rate for that category. Time:0.5 hour
  • The major with the highest percentage of full-time employment. Time:0.5 hour
